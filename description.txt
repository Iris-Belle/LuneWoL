Makes Terraria just a little bit worse to play...

changelog:
See the changelog tab on this steam item.

Fully customisable in advanced settings for those who love customising mods to an insane level.
Everything is disabled by default.

Credits:
Iris: Programmer, Professional Insane girl, Artist

Currently includes:
[list]
[*] [b]Darker Nights:[/b] Makes nights darker.
[*] [b]Wind Affects Arrows:[/b] Arrows are affected by wind.
[*] [b]Demon Mode:[/b] Silly meme
[*] [b]Chilly:[/b] Being in the tundra without warmth inflicts debuffs.
[*] [b]Hell Is Hot:[/b] Being in the underworld inflicts the On-Fire debuff.
[*] [b]No Evil Nighttime:[/b] Being in an evil biome at night rapidly depletes health.
[*] [b]Space Vacuum:[/b] Being in space will kill you slowly.
[*] [b]Dangerous Weathers:[/b] Some Weathers give debuffs and visual effects.
[*] [b]Worse Healing Potions:[/b] Healing potions heal less.
[*] [b]Disable Auto-Reuse:[/b] No more auto reuse.
[*] [b]No Accessories:[/b] No more accessories.
[*] [b]Reforge Nerf:[/b] Reforge buffs are cut down.
[*] [b]Recipe Ingredient %:[/b] Crafting takes more ingredients.
[*] [b]Fragile Ores:[/b] Makes the yield of ores worse.
[*] [b]Ore Density:[/b] World generates with less ores.
[*] [b]Buy Multiplier:[/b] Stuff costs more.
[*] [b]Sell Multiplier:[/b] Stuff sells for less.
[*] [b]Invasion Multiplier:[/b] Makes invasions require more kills.
[*] [b]Never Gold Enough:[/b] Enemies can never drop gold.
[*] [b]Less Money From Enemies:[/b] Enemies drop less money.
[*] [b]Murky Water:[/b] Makes it more difficult to see in water.
[*] [b]Water Depth Pressure:[/b] The deeper you are in a water body the faster you lose breath, with some other effects.
[*] [b]Viscous Water:[/b] Caps movement speed in water.
[*] [b]Water Poison:[/b] Water in biomes inflict debuffs.
[*] [b]Death Penalty:[/b] Max HP decreases on each death.
[*] [b]Item Despawn Timer:[/b] Despawns items.
[/list]
